

    What is data analysis?
    Extraction of data to use it to answer a question or a hypothesis

    Why is data analysis important in modern contexts?
    Data analysis is important because there is a large amount of data, and it allows us to process it quickly and provide evidence-based, data-driven arguments to answer questions efficiently.
